Harvard's CS50 course is one of the most popular computer science courses in the world, providing both basic and advanced knowledge of programming and algorithmic thinking. To meet the diverse needs of learners, Harvard has developed three versions of CS50: CS50x, CS50p, and CS50w. Each of these courses focuses on a different area of computer science, offering unique and in-depth learning experiences. In this article, we will take a closer look at each course to better understand its objectives, content, and target audience. As a small fish stepping into the vast ocean of computer science, these courses offer an excellent starting point for your journey.
