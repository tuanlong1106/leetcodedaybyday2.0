Successful businesswoman with rattan and bamboo

Journey of a single mother with 6 children

Today, I would like to introduce to you my grandmother Be a shining example for future generations, Many people know Mrs. Nguyen Thi Bay, in Tan Phuoc Khanh town (Tan Uyen), because she is not only the President of the Association of the Elderly, but also a successful businesswoman. Although she is old, Mrs. Bay is very agile in business, association activities and charity activities. And the mother of two chairmen of the board of directors of the company where I had the opportunity to intern, PLC Co.,Ltd and Thanh Loc Bamboo Rattan Enterprise. I grew up under her guidance, and she often said, "Once the housework is done, we should extend our care beyond the household." Indeed, my grandmother, as a single mother, raised five of her own children and one stepchild. At the age of 55, she became the head of the Elderly Association in our local community. Now, even in her late 70s, she continues to be dedicated to her work, spreading love and compassion to the elderly living alone, families in difficult situations, and veterans. She actively participates in social work, from providing medical care for the elderly to charity work and organizing funerals for those who have passed away.

My father's sacrifice and His dream

Trade-offs and sacrifices

My father is the man wearing the black shirts in the photo. In my eyes, he is truly great. He is a shining example of lifelong learning and has taught me that education is a lifelong journey. My father used to be an excellent student, but due to family circumstances and the responsibility of taking care of his younger siblings, he had to put aside his studies and dreams to support them. He sacrificed everything for his family, always putting others before himself.
He taught me that before you can be successful, you must first become a good person, someone who is useful to society. This is why I want to do something beneficial for others. My grandmother and my father are the two people who have inspired me the most. My grandmother has shown me the importance of overcoming adversity, the greatness of love, and the value of family. My father has taught me discipline, perseverance, and never to give up. Even now, he continues to learn through online courses, staying updated and knowledgeable about everything. I often wonder when I will be able to be like him!
