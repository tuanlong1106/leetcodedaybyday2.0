Phuc Loc Chau Co., Ltd

Phuc Loc Chau Co., Ltd. (PLC) is a company specializing in the production and trading of cardboard and carton boxes. Specifically, PLC focuses on the manufacturing and processing of high-quality carton boxes, providing tailored packaging solutions for various industries. One of our notable clients is ESTEC, a company known for producing speaker equipment for TESLA. Through our partnership with ESTEC, we ensure the supply of custom carton boxes that meet the high standards required for the safe and efficient packaging of their products. Our commitment to quality and precision has established PLC as a trusted partner in the packaging industry.

TLT Bamboo Rattan Enterprise

TLT Bamboo Rattan Enterprise is the cherished legacy of my grandmother, established before the year 2000. As one of the oldest companies in the region, we are proud to be a leading traditional craft enterprise in Binh Duong province. Recently, we have restructured the company to modernize and adapt to the current market, while preserving and sustaining the traditional craft village heritage of our province. Our company remains a top representative of this rich cultural tradition in Binh Duong
