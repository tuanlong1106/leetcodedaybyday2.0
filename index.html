    • "WE NEED YOU 🫵🏻"

    We believe that every developer has the potential to thrive if given the right opportunities and necessary support. With more than 5 years of experience in the field of Human Resources Management (HRM), I clearly understand the importance of connecting and supporting each other in the community. Therefore, "leetcodedayby" will no longer just be a place to share exercises, but will become a comprehensive platform with new features coming soon:

  • Sharing LeetCode knowledge and algorithms: Provide materials, articles, and detailed explanations of LeetCode problems and related algorithms. The goal is to help programmers enhance their problem-solving skills and prepare effectively for technical interviews.
  • Connecting candidates and businesses: Create a platform that connects programmers with businesses looking for talent. This includes offering job opportunities, supporting the recruitment process, and facilitating matches between programmers and suitable job positions.
  • Increasing employment rates for students and offering outsourcing solutions: Support recent graduates in finding jobs through internship opportunities and entry-level positions. Additionally, provide outsourcing solutions for small and medium-sized companies in the country, helping them address technological challenges and product development needs at an affordable cost.
